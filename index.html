Franklin For Commissioner

Hello my name is Franklin Gomez Flores,

 Up until March 3rd I was petitioning for the opportunity to appear on the November ballot as a candidate for Chatham County Commissioner - District 5. I was required to obtain 2,148 signatures from registered voters in Chatham County. Thanks to my team and dedicated volunteers, we were able to collect over 3,500 signatures! And now, I am a certified candidate for Chatham County Commissioner - District 5.
